The Planning Desk

Book it, in the right order

Everything that needs a reservation, counted back from departure (Fri, Apr 9, 2027Fri, May 7, 2027). Dates are safe targets — earlier is always better for the scarce items: the small B&Bs, the balloon, the private boat, and peak-time flights. The season toggle above updates every date on this page.

As soon as you commit

Now

Check both passports — valid well past the return date (Schengen requires 3+ months beyond departure; renew now if it’s close — US renewals can take weeks).

Now

Set flight fare alerts for the open-jaw (SAT → SUF, PSA → SAT) on Google Flights or Kayak. Price Houston (IAH) as an alternative origin — often more options.

By Dec 2026

Set up the honeymoon registry (Honeyfund, Zola) and tie it to the Feb 2027 wedding invites — see the gift ideas below.

~6 months out — lock the scarce items

By Nov 2026By Dec 2026

Final 1–2 weeks

By ~Apr 2By ~Apr 30

Buy eSIM data plans for both phones.

Final week

Reconfirm everything — hotels, both car pickups, the balloon / boat / driver. Confirm the last Salerno → Amalfi ferry time for your Monday, and the San Miniato vespers schedule. Download the Trenitalia, Italo, ferry, and offline-map apps.

Anytime

Regional trains can wait — Florence → Lucca and Lucca → Pisa are fixed-price; buy on the app even day-of.

—

No booking needed: Villa Cimbrone gardens (pay at the gate) · the Orange Garden and Aventine keyhole (free) · San Miniato vespers (free) · the Vitaleta sunrise (free) · most trattoria lunches.

April note: your last full day and departure (Apr 24–25) fall on Italy’s Liberation Day weekend — expect ceremonies and some closures in Lucca; the Pisa flight is unaffected. Also confirm coastal hotels, the Salerno–Amalfi ferries, and the boat operators have opened for the season.
May note: the coast is fully in season — which is exactly why Atrani, the balloon, and the private boat are the first things to sell out. Book those three before anything else.
